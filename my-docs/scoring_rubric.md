# Scoring rubric (human evaluation)

Score each category from 1–5.

| Category | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Meaning accuracy | Wrong | Partial | Exact |
| Completeness | Missing ideas | Minor loss | Full |
| Fluency | Broken | Acceptable | Native-like |
| Register & tone | Wrong | Mixed | Formal literary |
| Metaphor handling | Lost | Simplified | Preserved |
| Grammar | Many errors | Few | Clean |

**Final score**: Total = sum / 30

**Interpretation**:
- 26–30 → Excellent
- 20–25 → Good
- 14–19 → Weak
- <14 → Fail

Use this rubric as a guide for human raters. Record raw scores per-category and compute the averaged final score for comparisons with automatic mappings.
